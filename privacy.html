Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

Bluepeak Financial is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our services.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly:

  • Account registration information (name, email, phone, address)
  • Identity verification documents (government ID, proof of address)
  • Financial information (bank account details, tax information)
  • Investment preferences and risk profile
  • Communication preferences

Information Collected Automatically:

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages you visit and time spent
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies
  • Transaction history and activity logs

3. How We Use Your Information

Bluepeak Financial uses your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve investment management services
  • To process transactions and manage your account
  • To send periodic reports and statements
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • To prevent fraud and security issues
  • To communicate with you about your account
  • To send marketing communications (with your consent)
  • To conduct market research and improve our services

4. Data Security

Bluepeak Financial implements industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • 256-bit SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Multi-factor authentication (MFA) for account access
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Secure data storage in encrypted databases
  • Restricted access to personal information
  • Annual cybersecurity compliance certifications

Despite our best efforts, no security system is impenetrable. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

5. Information Sharing

Bluepeak Financial does not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Third parties who assist with our operations (custodians, auditors, payment processors)
  • Legal Requirements: Government agencies and law enforcement when required by law
  • Regulatory Compliance: Financial regulators and compliance authorities
  • Business Partners: Only with your explicit consent

All third parties are contractually bound to maintain confidentiality and use your information only for agreed purposes.

6. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Bluepeak Financial uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality
  • Analytical Cookies: Help us understand how you use our services
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Marketing Cookies: Used for targeted advertising (with consent)

You can control cookies through your browser settings, though disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correction: Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Portability: Request transfer of your information to another provider
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of marketing communications anytime

To exercise any of these rights, contact our Privacy Team at privacy@bluepeakfinancial.com.

8. Data Retention

Bluepeak Financial retains your information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide investment services and manage your account
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements

After your account closure, we retain information for minimum 7 years as required by financial regulations. Information used for marketing is retained until you opt out.

9. Children's Privacy

Bluepeak Financial does not knowingly collect information from children under 18 years of age. Our services are not directed to minors. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child, we will promptly delete such information.

10. Changes to This Policy

Bluepeak Financial may update this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify you of material changes via email or by posting a prominent notice on our website. Your continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
