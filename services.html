Portfolio Diversification Strategy

Our sophisticated approach to risk management through diversification.

Asset Class Diversification

Equities (Stocks): 55-60%

  • 25% Large-cap global leaders
  • 20% Mid-cap growth companies
  • 10-15% Emerging markets

Fixed Income (Bonds): 35-40%

  • 15% Government bonds (AAA-rated)
  • 15% Investment-grade corporate bonds
  • 5-10% High-yield bonds (diversified)

Geographic Diversification

We spread investments across multiple regions to reduce geographic risk:

  • 30% North America
  • 28% Europe
  • 20% Asia-Pacific
  • 12% Emerging Markets
  • 10% Other Regions

This diversification helps ensure that poor performance in one region is balanced by stability in others.
