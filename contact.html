Common Questions

Find quick answers to frequently asked questions.

How do I open an account?

Simply click "Get Started" on our homepage or fill out the contact form. Our team will send you account opening instructions and all necessary documentation within 24 hours.

What information do I need to provide?

We require valid government ID, proof of address, bank information for transfers, and tax information (W-9 or equivalent for US investors). Everything is handled securely online.

How do I deposit funds?

You can transfer funds via bank wire, ACH transfer, credit card, or cryptocurrency (Premium members). Instructions are provided in your account setup email.

Can I get investment advice?

All clients receive portfolio guidance based on their risk profile. Premium members have dedicated account managers who provide personalized investment advice.

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fees or hidden charges. You only pay our transparent annual management fee, which is disclosed upfront and varies by plan.

How is my money kept safe?

Your funds are held with independent custodians (never with Bluepeak). All accounts are insured and we maintain strict security protocols and compliance standards.
